PrivateCircle Markets

AI-Powered Fundraising & Deal-Flow Marketplace

PrivateCircle Markets is an AI-powered private market platform designed to help startups connect with investors and enable investors to discover, evaluate, and manage investment opportunities through data-driven insights.

Role Product Design | UX/UI Design
Industry Fintech | Private Markets
Platform Web Application
Users Founders, Investors, HNIs

Project Overview

Private market fundraising involves complex workflows where founders need access to relevant investors, while investors need reliable company intelligence, deal insights, and efficient ways to evaluate opportunities.

The goal was to design a platform that simplifies fundraising discovery, improves investor-founder connections, and creates a seamless journey from profile discovery to meeting completion.

Product Goals
  • Create an efficient marketplace connecting founders and investors
  • Improve investor discovery and matching experience
  • Enable data-driven investment decisions
  • Simplify fundraising workflows
  • Provide transparency across deal progress and communication
My Role & Responsibilities

Product Design | UX/UI Design

  • Defined product features and user workflows across investor and founder journeys
  • Designed high-fidelity interfaces and end-to-end product experiences
  • Created user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and design systems
  • Collaborated with product managers, developers, and business teams
  • Conducted UX reviews and usability improvements
  • Validated designs for feasibility and scalable implementation

Detailed Case Study

Section 1

AI-Powered Fundraising Marketplace

Designed a marketplace experience where startups can discover investors and investors can explore fundraising opportunities.

  • Investor & fundraiser profiles
  • AI-powered founder matching
  • Company intelligence & insights
  • Deal-flow discovery & tracking
Section 2

Investor Profiles & Matching

Designed investor profile experiences to help founders identify relevant investors based on investment sectors, ticket size, portfolio history, investment preferences, and location.

Section 3

Fundraiser Dashboard

Created a centralized dashboard experience for founders to manage investor connections, meeting requests, deal progress, investor conversations, and fundraising activities.

Section 4

Investor Dashboard

Designed investor workflows to manage startup discovery, saved opportunities, portfolio tracking, active connections, and meeting schedules.

Section 5

Investor Profiles & Deal Insights

Created detailed investor and company profile experiences with structured information, investment history, sectors, keywords, and deal insights.

Section 6

Meeting Scheduling Experience

Designed a complete investor-founder meeting workflow including meeting requests, time slot selection, confirmation, calendar scheduling, and completion flow.

Section 7

Signup & Onboarding Experience

Redesigned onboarding to create a faster and clearer entry experience.

  • Registration & company details
  • Founder account completion
